Frequently Asked Questions

Here are answers to some common questions about Vaagai Meter Auto, including fare details, booking process, and special support services like free travel for pregnant women.

1. How much is the base fare for a Vaagai Meter Auto ride?

The base fare is ₹59 for the first 1.5 km, which includes ₹40 for the ride and ₹19 as a convenience fee. After that, it's ₹18 per km.

2. Is travel really free for pregnant women?

Yes, absolutely! Vaagai Meter Auto provides free rides for pregnant women within Madurai city limits. Our trained drivers ensure comfort and priority service for all such passengers.

3. How do I book a ride?

You can book a ride instantly through our mobile app available on Google Play. Real-time tracking and estimated time of arrival (ETA) are included for every ride.

4. Do you charge extra at night?

Yes, a night fare surcharge of +50% applies between 10:01 PM and 5:59 AM. All charges are calculated digitally using GPS for transparency.

5. What safety measures do you follow?

All our drivers are verified and trained. Rides are tracked via GPS, and payments are secure. Our 24/7 customer support team is always available for assistance.
